Farmers Schemes

PRADHAN MANTRI FASAL BIMA YOJANA (PMFBY)

To provide insurance coverage and financial support to the farmers in the event of failure of any of the notified crop as a result of natural calamities, pests & diseases.

Click here : PMFBY

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana

An assured monthly pension of Rs. 3000/- to all the small and marginal farmers (who own cultivable land up to 2 hectares) after the age of 60 years.

Click here to apply:Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan Dhan Yojana

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana

Under the Scheme an amount of Rs.6000/- per year is released by the Central Government

E-NAM

E-NAM is an electronic National Agriculture Marketing portal that pans India and connects the existing mandis. The purpose is to create a pan India market for agricultural produce.
